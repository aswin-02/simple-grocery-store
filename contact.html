FAQ
  • How do I contact customer support ?


    You can reach our customer support team through various channels. We provide a dedicated customer support phone number, email address, and live chat feature on our website. Our knowledgeable representatives will be happy to assist you with any questions, concerns, or issues you may have.

  • Can I track my delivery?


    Yes, you can track your delivery through our website or mobile app. Once your order is out for delivery, you will receive updates regarding the status and estimated time of arrival. You can also contact our customer support team for any additional assistance.

  • Can I return or exchange items?


    Yes, we have a return and exchange policy in place. If you are unsatisfied with an item, please contact our customer support within a specified time frame, usually within 24 to 48 hours of delivery. We will assist you with the return or exchange process based on the specific circumstances.

    We strive to keep our inventory up to date, but occasionally, items may be out of stock due to high demand or other factors.
